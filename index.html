About

A shepherd’s heart, a leader’s mind.

Apostle Dr. Samuel Letang is the Senior Pastor of Tlokweng Assembly of God in Botswana, where he serves alongside his wife, Magdalene Letang. Together they lead a community-based, missional Pentecostal congregation with a vision for revival, community transformation, and global missions.

A lifelong servant of the Church, Dr. Letang has given decades to the Assemblies of God in Botswana — pastoring, teaching, and raising up the next generation of ministers. He served as President of Assembly Bible College, shaping the formation of leaders across the nation, and has taught as an Instructor of Bible with Global University’s School for Ministry Development. He remains a sought-after preacher at national conferences and leadership gatherings.

Beyond the pulpit, he is widely respected as a teacher on leadership — championing transformational, Christ-centred leadership that equips people to walk in their God-given calling. His ministry is marked by a simple conviction: “Our heart is to see people come to know Christ, grow in their faith, and fulfill their God-given purpose.”

The Journey

Decades of faithful service

A calling that has grown from the local pulpit to the formation of leaders across Botswana.

  1. 2001

    First called to pastor

    His journey in ministry begins with a call to shepherd God’s people — stepping into pastoral ministry within the Assemblies of God in Botswana and giving himself fully to a local congregation.

  2. Formation

    Theological foundations

    Answering the call, he deepens his preparation — earning his Bachelor’s and Master’s degrees through Global University, pursuing studies in Christian Spirituality at the University of South Africa (UNISA), and going on to complete doctoral studies.

  3. 2007

    Called to Gaborone

    Transferred to the capital to help lead Assembly Bible College — turning from shepherding a flock to forming those who would shepherd many.

  4. 2009–2015

    President, Assembly Bible College

    Leads the national Bible College for six years, equipping pastors, evangelists, and ministry leaders for the church in Botswana and beyond.

  5. 2018

    National Conference Preacher

    Ministers at the Assemblies of God National Conference — “Rekindling the Spirit of Faith” — a recognised voice to the wider movement.

  6. Today

    Senior Pastor, Tlokweng Assembly of God

    Leads a thriving congregation with his wife Magdalene, mentoring leaders and carrying a vision of “Reaching the Lost & Equipping the Found.”

Looking Up to Master Jesus

A devotional invitation to fix your eyes on Jesus — and be changed.

In his forthcoming book, Apostle Dr. Samuel Letang turns the reader’s gaze upward — to Jesus, the Author and Perfecter of our faith. Drawing on decades of pastoral ministry and the formation of leaders, he offers a Christ-centred vision for living, leading, and growing through every season.

Part devotional, part leadership reflection, Looking Up to Master Jesus calls believers beyond merely receiving from God to faithfully maintaining and multiplying what they have received — with the Master always in full view.
